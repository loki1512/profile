POWER SYSTEMS

Enhancement of Distribution System Performance with Reconfiguration, Distributed Generation and Capacitor Bank Deployment

Grey Wolf Optimizer (GWO) inspired by hierarchical hunting dynamics

This research explored how complex electrical distribution networks can be optimized using intelligent, nature-inspired search strategies.

The work utilized the Grey Wolf Optimizer (GWO), a bio-inspired optimization algorithm modeled after the coordinated hunting behavior and hierarchical decision-making patterns observed in grey wolves.

The system searched for efficient network configurations involving distributed generation, capacitor placement and feeder reconfiguration to improve voltage stability and reduce losses across the distribution network.

Optimization Power Systems Distributed Generation Network Reconfiguration Capacitor Placement Bio-Inspired Optimization

What It Taught Me

Nature often solves problems through adaptive collective behavior rather than rigid centralized control.

The Grey Wolf Optimizer demonstrated how decentralized exploration and coordinated convergence can efficiently navigate extremely large optimization spaces.

The research reinforced the idea that intelligent systems frequently emerge from interaction patterns rather than brute-force computation alone.

MEDICAL AI

A Deep Learning Model for Diagnosing Diabetic Retinopathy Using EfficientNet-B0

This research focused on building a deployable diabetic retinopathy detection system for low-resource healthcare environments rather than pursuing computationally expensive benchmark models.

EfficientNet-B0 was deliberately chosen not because it was the largest model, but because it offered an optimal balance between accuracy, computational efficiency and real-world deployability on constrained hardware such as Raspberry Pi systems.

Deep Learning Computer Vision Medical AI Healthcare EfficientNet-B0 Edge AI Deployable ML Resource-Constrained AI

What It Taught Me

Accuracy alone is not enough. A system becomes valuable only when it remains usable under real-world constraints such as limited hardware, deployment cost and accessibility.

The project reinforced the importance of designing intelligent systems that balance performance, efficiency and practical deployment.

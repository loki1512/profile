Understanding Complex Systems

I view research as a structured way of understanding systems.

Understanding Complex Systems

At first glance, power system optimization and diabetic retinopathy diagnosis appear unrelated.

One studies electrical networks. The other studies retinal images.

Yet both required the same mindset: understanding complexity, identifying meaningful patterns and using evidence to guide decisions.

RESEARCH PHILOSOPHY

Reducing Difficulty

I am interested in systems where information exists, but understanding is difficult.

Research, to me, is the process of reducing that difficulty.

LOOKING AHEAD

Questions I Continue To Explore

How can optimization improve real-world decision systems?
How can analytics become more accessible to small businesses?
How can educational systems adapt to learners?

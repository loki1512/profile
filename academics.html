CONVERGENCE

Where The Two Paths Meet

VIT taught me to see systems.

Concepts such as power system operation, economic dispatch, unit commitment, optimization, and network reconfiguration taught me to think simultaneously at the macro and micro levels.

IIT Madras taught me to see information.

Machine learning, statistics, business data management, and analytics revealed how patterns emerge from data and how decisions can be improved through evidence.

Together, these perspectives shaped my work.

The combination became visible in my work. Optimization and systems thinking shaped my power systems research, while machine learning and analytical modeling supported my medical imaging research.

The same combination continues to influence KSA 3.0, Quantum Quiz, CATTRACK, educational technology platforms, and future interests in decision intelligence, supply chain analytics, and quantum computing.
